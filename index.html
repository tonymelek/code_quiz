You have 7 minutes to finish a quiz of 14 questions. if you answered a question incorrectly, 30 seconds will be deducted from the countdown timer at upper right corner of the screen.

  • If you got more than 7 questions correct, you will be granted a weighted bonus.
  • Quiz ends when you have answered all the questions or countdown timer reaches zero.
  • After you finish, you can choose to add your name to Hall of Fame and/or re-start the quiz.
Note: The questions and choices will be shuffled every time you play.
